Quick Setup Guide

1
Download & Transfer: Download the APK above on a phone/computer and send it to your Android TV (using a USB drive or local sharing apps like Send Files to TV).
2
Install: Open the APK on your TV and install it. Allow installation from unknown sources if prompted.
3
Connect: Open the app, type in the server URL (e.g. https://citylife.kooker.co.za), and hit connect.
4
Peripherals: Pair a PlayStation/Bluetooth Controller or connect a keyboard & mouse to navigate the 3D world and take control of the citizens.
